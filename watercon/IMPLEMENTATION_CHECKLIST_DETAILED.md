### If Implementation Delays Occur

- [ ] **DELAY-001** | If equipment delivery delays by >4 weeks
  - Action: Expedite shipping (airfreight); negotiate expedited manufacturing with supplier
  - Cost impact: +₹20-30 Lakhs
  - Timeline adjustment: +2-4 weeks per delayed item

- [ ] **DELAY-002** | If site civil works delayed (excavation, foundation delays)
  - Action: Prioritize critical path items (tanks, MBR basin); defer non-critical installations
  - Parallel deployment: Pursue non-site-dependent items (metering, controls, training)
  - Timeline adjustment: +4-8 weeks

### If Performance Shortfalls Occur

- [ ] **PERFORM-001** | If water reduction <30% achieved by Month 6
  - Action: Additional leak audit; identify overlooked high-loss areas
  - Consider accelerated Phase 2 deployment (e.g., MBR earlier than planned)
  - Review operational parameter tuning (may need more aggressive CoC settings)

- [ ] **PERFORM-002** | If recirculated water quality issues emerge (e.g., PM product contamination)
  - Action: Upgrade tertiary polishing (UV/ozone treatment)
  - Divert affected reuse stream to lower-quality end-use (irrigation vs. process)
  - Additional capital: ₹25-40 Lakhs

- [ ] **PERFORM-003** | If membrane fouling exceeds expected CIP frequency
  - Action: Increase pre-filtration robustness (finer screens)
  - Reduce recirculation flow rate (accept smaller water recovery short-term)
  - Plan for membrane replacement (3-year life becomes 2-year)

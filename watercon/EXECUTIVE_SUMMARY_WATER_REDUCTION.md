## WATER REUSE CASCADE MODEL

```
TIER 1: Highest Quality
  ↓ DM Water (post-use)
  ↓ Hot Condensate
  └→ Cooling Tower / Low-grade wash

TIER 2: Medium Quality  
  ↓ Drainage water (filtered)
  ↓ Paper Machine condensate (polished)
  └→ Pulping / Process / Dust suppression

TIER 3: Low Quality
  ↓ Bagasse effluent (treated)
  ↓ Cooling tower blowdown (settled)
  └→ Irrigation / Recharge ponds

TIER 4: Final Disposal/Recovery
  ↓ Brine/Irretrievable streams
  └→ Evaporation ponds / Controlled discharge
```

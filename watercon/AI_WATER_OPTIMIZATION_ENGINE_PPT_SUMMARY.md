## SLIDE 5: AREA 1 - LEAKAGE DETECTION & ARRESTING (CRITICAL)

**The Problem:**
- Storage tanks & pipelines losing 3-5% (vs. industry standard <2%)
- Monthly Loss: 99,280 m³ (3.8% of total drawl)

**The Solution:**
1. Install water meters at intake + 5 drainage points
2. Acoustic leak detection specialist analysis
3. Pressure testing on pipelines >10 years old
4. Night-time flow analysis (2-4 AM) baseline
5. Repair with epoxy coatings + PENETRON compound
6. Automatic leak detection alarms

**Impact:**
- Target: Reduce 4% → <1% system loss
- Water Saved: **1.58 m³/tonne**
- Timeline: **3 months**
- CapEx: **₹80-120 Lakhs**

## SLIDE 6: AREA 2 - DRAINAGE WATER REUSE & RECOVERY (CRITICAL)

**The Problem:**
- Chemical Recovery (200k), Paper Machines (80-100k), Bagasse (50k), DM rejects (50k)
- Total Waste: 350,000 m³/month to drainage with NO reuse

**The Solution - 3-Line Segregation:**
1. **High-Quality Water** → Domestic toilets/urinals (saves 50k m³)
2. **Medium-Quality Water** → Cooling tower makeup after filtration (60-80k m³)
3. **Mixed Drainage** → Membrane Bioreactor (200k m³) treatment for bagasse pulping

**Implementation:**
- Create segregated 3-line network
- 3 isolation tanks (1,000 m³ each)
- Deploy Membrane Bioreactor

**Impact:**
- Recycled: **250,000-340,000 m³/month**
- Water Saved: **4.67 m³/tonne**
- Timeline: **8 months**
- CapEx: **₹200-300 Lakhs**

## SLIDE 7: AREA 3 - THERMAL ENERGY RECOVERY

**The Problem:**
- Chemical Recovery, Paper Machines discharge at 70-85°C
- Only 20-25% heat recovered → **55% thermal recovery gap**
- Waste: 165,000 m³ thermal potential

**The Solution:**
1. Install Plate Heat Exchangers (PHE) at 3-4 sources
2. Cool effluent 75-85°C → 25-30°C
3. Recover 45-55°C differential
4. Submerged Microfiltration (0.2 micron) on 200k m³
5. Polish with RO for boiler feedwater reuse

**Impact:**
- Fresh Water Displacement: **120,000-150,000 m³/month**
- Water Saved: **2.75 m³/tonne**
- Timeline: **10 months**
- CapEx: **₹150-250 Lakhs**

- **Problem:** Wet depithing uses 40-50% inefficiently

## SLIDE 11: AREA 9 - CIRCULAR ECONOMY & ZLD PATH

**The Problem:**
- Linear Model: Fresh intake → Use → Discharge
- 28% Wastewater wasted (698,000 m³/month)

**4-TIER CASCADE REUSE MODEL:**

**TIER 1 (Highest Quality):**
- DM post-use → Low-grade wash / Cooling makeup
- Hot condensate → Heat extraction → Reuse

**TIER 2 (Medium Quality):**
- Filtered drainage → Paper machine / Cooling tower
- Polished condensate → Dust suppression / Irrigation

**TIER 3 (Low Quality):**
- MBR-treated bagasse effluent → Irrigation / Recharge ponds
- CBD + cooling blowdown → Holding pond → Evaporation

**TIER 4 (Final):**
- Irretrievable brine → Evaporation / Controlled discharge

**Impact:**
- **11.63 m³/tonne reduction**
- **85%+ wastewater recycled**
- **ZLD certification path**
- Timeline: **12 months**
- CapEx: **₹500-700 Lakhs**

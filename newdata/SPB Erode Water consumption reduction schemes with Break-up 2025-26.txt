Rain Water Harvesting -Capture & Conserve for substituting Clarified River water.

Areas Successfully Practised in SPB Mill
* Rain water 
Rain water as available more during rainy season & less /occasionally during non-rainy season is diverted to a storage pond & preserved for use in mill -process as high quality water.

* RO rejects 
RO rejects water [ DM plant complex] is to be reused as low grade water in select areas of paper mill.

* Cooling water in Captive Power Plant
It  shall be conditioned so as to reduce the blow-down & hence lowering make-up fresh water quantity on a continuous period.

* Hot condensate from Paper machine   
It cannot be used as such in CPP as boiler feed water. Instead of draining [  calling for increased DM water quantity from additional river water drawl] the same, the hot condensate is cooled to low temperature in Plate Heat Exchangers. The cooled condensate is polished through active carbon filter & DM plant as DM water & the latter is passed through other side of heat exchanger for heating the same. The hot purified clean condensate is then used as feed water to the power boiler.

* Effluent discharge
Effluent discharge from Pulping Complex is not drained ;instead the effluents are led to the sugar-cane plantation /field in  place of water used for irrigation/plantation. No fresh manure needs to be added, as the effluent is already rich in nutrients.   

* Boiler Blow-down reduction & reuse.
The blow-down quantity -though small- is reduced through advanced polyamine dosing of Boiler feed water of very high quality. Even the small amount of CBD could be reused in some other areas of the plant.

* Water Leakage
Storage Tanks sometime starts leaking on a continuous basis
In order to contain the same , patch -up work with PENETRON compound for permanent leakage closure.

* 3 Stages of Water Reuse
To start with the highest quality DM water after use gets contaminated to an extent; the same can be reused in low grade water required areas including that for Rest rooms/Toilets. Cooling water blow-down from Cooling tower shall be directed to select areas of Paper mill thus displacing fresh clean water usage.

Water used in pulping process would become effluent & shall be diverted for irrigation after partial treatment of the effluent -including lowering the temperature through waste heat recovery.

In PCC, the fouled condensate could be used instead of fresh water.
